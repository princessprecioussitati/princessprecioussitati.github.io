Emberharbour Ecosystem Overview

The Hurdles We Face (Market Problems)

  • Price-Quality Gap Finding "cheap" goods often means sacrificing quality, while premium items are overpriced due to middlemen.
  • Fragmented Real Estate Finding housing requires physical travel and dealing with unverified brokers, incurring high costs.
  • Unreliable Access Domestic help is found via word-of-mouth with no standard for vetting, safety, or fair pricing.

An Integrated Lifestyle Hub (The Solution)

  • Curated E-Commerce Verified suppliers offering high-quality goods delivered directly home.
  • Virtual Real Estate Localized directory with verified photos and digital tours to eliminate scouting travel.
  • On-Demand Services A marketplace for vetted domestic professionals like cleaners, babysitters, and chefs.

Core Value Propositions

  • Hyper-Local Focus Fast delivery and localized providers unlike global platforms.
  • Cost Efficiency Optimized logistics and reduced broker layers keep prices low.
  • Trust & Safety Vetted listings and providers protect you from fraud.

Operational Framework

  • Technology Secure mobile app & web platform with integrated payments.
  • Logistics Motorcycle-based delivery network for "comfort of home" speed.
  • Partnerships Local wholesalers and real estate developers.

Monetization & Pricing Model

  • Retail Commission Transaction fees on each retail sale.
  • Listing Fees Charged to real estate agents for premium visibility.
  • Service Percentage Percentage from service provider fees for jobs completed.
